Computer Science Description

The Computer Science program at Saveetha College is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, programming languages, and software development. The course covers a wide range of topics, including algorithms, data structures, computer networks, and database systems.

Computer Science Objectives

  • Understand the fundamental principles of computer science
  • Develop programming skills in languages such as Java, Python, and C++
  • Design and develop software applications
  • Analyze and solve complex computational problems
