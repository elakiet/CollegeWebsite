Economics Description

The Economics program at Saveetha College is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of economic principles and theories. The course covers a wide range of topics, including microeconomics, macroeconomics, international trade, and economic development.

Economics Objectives

  • Understand the fundamental principles of economics
  • Analyze economic data and trends
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Apply economic theories to real-world scenarios
