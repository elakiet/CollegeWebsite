Course Description

The English program at Saveetha College is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in English language and literature. The course covers a wide range of topics, including grammar, composition, literature, and critical thinking.

Course Objectives

  • Develop reading, writing, and speaking skills in English
  • Analyze and interpret literary texts
  • Develop critical thinking and analytical skills
  • Improve communication skills through group discussions and presentations
