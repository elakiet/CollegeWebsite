Course Description

The sociology program at Saveetha College is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in sociology language. The course covers a wide range of topics, including grammar, composition, literature, and critical thinking. This comprehensive program aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in their academic and professional pursuits.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the fundamental concepts of sociology
  • Analyze social structures and institutions
  • Develop critical thinking and analytical skills
  • Improve research and communication skills
